# Phase 02: Live-Intel Room Navigation - Plan

Enhance the list-based room navigation within the Nervalis terminal to provide real-time strategic intelligence and improved UX.

## Wave 1: Presence & Awareness (UI/UX)
Focuses on visual intelligence and student tracking.

### Task 1: Hover Presence Tooltips
- **Action**: Implement a floating tooltip/overlay that appears when hovering over a room in the `AcademyMap` list.
- **Content**: Display the sprites/images of students currently in the room.
- **Files**:
  - `src/features/exploration/components/AcademyMap.tsx`
- **Acceptance Criteria**: Hovering over a room shows avatars of occupants.

### Task 2: "Current Room" Highlighting
- **Action**: Mark the room where the user's character is currently located with a distinct blue glow and "YOU ARE HERE" indicator.
- **Files**:
  - `src/features/exploration/components/AcademyMap.tsx`
- **Acceptance Criteria**: The current room is visually distinct from other navigable rooms.

### Task 3: Maintenance & Privacy Feedback
- **Action**: Add a "Glitch" animation/feedback when hovering or clicking a room in Maintenance (blocked). Ensure Private rooms remain clickable.
- **Files**:
  - `src/features/exploration/components/AcademyMap.tsx`
- **Acceptance Criteria**: Maintenance rooms feel "offline" while Private rooms remain accessible.

## Wave 2: Filtering & Security
Focuses on student/staff role boundaries.

### Task 4: Hidden Rooms Logic
- **Action**: Ensure rooms marked as "Hidden" (via a specific flag or name convention like "ZONA_ALPHA") are not rendered for regular students but visible to Staff.
- **Files**:
  - `src/features/exploration/components/AcademyMap.tsx`
- **Acceptance Criteria**: Secret rooms are invisible to standard players in the terminal list.

## Verification Plan

### Automated Verification
- `npm run build` to ensure no type regressions in the new component logic.

### Manual Verification (UAT)
- **Intel Test**: Move a student to Room A, check Nervalis in Room B; verify student appears in Room A's entry.
- **Hover Test**: Hover over Room A; verify avatars appear in tooltip.
- **Current Room Test**: Confirm the room you are in is highlighted.
- **Security Test**: Verify secret rooms are only visible when logged in as Staff.
