# Phase 01.2: Debugging & Stabilization - Walkthrough

Resolved UI inconsistencies, system-wide bugs, and world-state synchronization issues.

## Changes Made

### 1. Notification & Interaction Stabilization
- **Notification Sync**: Added a `useEffect` safeguard in `NervalisOverlay.tsx` to ensure `hasUnreadSignals` is cleared immediately when the terminal is opened.
- **Layering Hierarchy**: 
  - Updated `PrivacyPollModal.tsx` and `StealthEntryDialog.tsx` to `z-[250]`.
  - Confirmed `NervalisOverlay.tsx` remains at `z-300` to take absolute priority.
- **Draggable Bounds**: Verified `dragConstraints` and `dragElastic={0}` settings to keep the terminal within the viewport.

### 2. Investigation System Hardening
- **Double-Voting Prevention**: 
  - Implemented `isVoting` state in `PollCard` within `EvidenceTab.tsx`.
  - Vote buttons now become disabled and display "VOTANDO..." during the async submission process.
- **Evidence Visibility Sync**:
  - Updated `resolvePoll` in `src/features/investigation/api.ts` to set `is_hidden: true` upon poll acceptance.
  - Updated `InsideRoomArena.tsx` to reactively filter out hidden evidence from the 3D scene.

### 3. Aesthetic Polish
- **CRT Effect**: Subtly adjusted CRT scanline opacity from `0.5` to `0.2` in `RoomPageClient.tsx` for a more premium, less intrusive visual experience.
